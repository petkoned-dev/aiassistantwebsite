05 / Logo

Logo Usage

The horizontal lockup is the primary brand mark. Always use the supplied file — never recreate the logo by hand.

On Backgrounds

On white — preferred
On violet-50 — approved
On dark — frosted container

Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space equal to the height of the robot character on all four sides. Never crowd the logo with competing elements.

Min width
160px
Min height
40px

Do & Don't

DO

Use on white or light violet backgrounds

DON'T

Stretch or distort proportions

DO

Scale proportionally, preserving aspect ratio

DON'T

Apply drop shadows directly to the logo

DO

Use a frosted panel on dark backgrounds

DON'T

Place on busy or patterned backgrounds

08 / Voice & Tone

Voice & Tone

What we say and how we say it shapes the brand as much as the logo does.

We are…

  • — Curious and exploratory
  • — Warm but not saccharine
  • — Confident, not arrogant
  • — Direct without being terse
  • — Slightly playful

We sound like…

  • — A knowledgeable friend
  • — A patient teacher
  • — A curious collaborator
  • — Someone who listens first
  • — An approachable expert

We never…

  • — Use unexplained jargon
  • — Sound robotic or sterile
  • — Condescend or lecture
  • — Oversell or hype
  • — Dismiss uncertainty

Copy in Practice

Not this

"Leverage our cutting-edge AI-powered solution to optimize your workflow efficiency."

Instead

"Ask anything. AiA helps you think faster and work smarter — in plain language."

Approved Vocabulary

Ask Explore Discover Clear Simple Friendly Helpful Curious Together Instantly Smart Warm Real Honest Thoughtful Approachable
